**Goal & Requirements**  
- Create a **single-page, mobile-first website** for the “AI Attorney” development team.  
- Use **Tailwind CSS** for styling (you can use the Tailwind CDN).  
- The purpose of this page is to attract fanatical talent to join team to change the legal industry.
- Include a hero section with a video background and a call to action button.
"The legal industry is broken. We are building the future of law."

**Content**
- A hero section with a video background and a call to action button.
- To apply people should fill out a form with their name, email, and a message.
- The form should be validated with JavaScript.
- The form should be submitted to the server with JavaScript.
- The server should send a confirmation email to the applicant.
- The website should be responsive and look good on all devices.
- The website should be hosted on Vercel.

**Design**
- The website should be modern and professional.
- The website should be easy to use and navigate.
- The website should be visually appealing and engaging.
- The website should be consistent in its design and branding.
